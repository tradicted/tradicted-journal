# Changelog

## v1.0.0 — Initial Release

- Risk-Reward Calculator with position sizing, R:R ratio, and breakeven win rate
- Trade log with sortable table, inline editing, and bulk delete
- Analytics dashboard with equity curve, monthly P&L, and win/loss charts
- Daily journal with calendar view and mood tracking
- New trade form with auto-calculated P&L and R-multiple
- CSV and JSON export
- Settings for default account size, risk %, and currency symbol
- SQLite local storage — fully offline, no account required
